Paris Saint-Germain Jumpman MVP

We didn't invent the remix—but considering the material we get to sample, this one's a no-brainer. We took elements from the AJ6, 7 and 8, making them into a completely new shoe that celebrates MJ's first 3-peat championship run. With leather, textile and nubuck details, these sneakers honor one legacy while encouraging you to cement your own. Benefits Upper is a mix of AJ6 and AJ7 elements: the tongue top and modified overlay reference the AJ6 while the semi-bootie lining homages the AJ7. Embroidered Nike Air heel logo references the AJ6 and the molded heel detail comes from the AJ8. Outsole design is a nod to the AJ6. Encapsulated Nike Air-Sole unit provide lightweight cushioning.
